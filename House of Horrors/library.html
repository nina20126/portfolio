Take a moment to relax with a book


We are very proud of our little horror library! Here you can find both novels and historical stories, all about some ghostly incidences! Explore what we have before your trip and get ready for some cold vibes! If you are visiting our hotel you don't need a library card to borrow our books.

Most popular books

  1. Interview with a Vampire, by Anne Rice
  2. The Legend of Sleepy Hollow, by Washington Irving
  3. Dracula, by Bram Stoker
  4. Pride and Prejudice and Zombies, by Jane Austen & Seth Grahame-Smith
  5. Necronomicon, by H. P. Lovecraft

Our recommendations:

  • Carmilla, by Sheridan Le Fanu

  • The Raven, by Edgar Allan Poe

  • American Gothic, by Jonathan Rigby
